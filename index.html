Background

As a cub scout den leader to a group of boys through their Tiger and Wolf ranks, I have learned that a good year-long plan is important. Being able to plan ahead allows better preparation and timing for the activities that make up this important program. As the Bear year begins, I wanted a place where I could visualize the meeting schedule and position items in the right place.
